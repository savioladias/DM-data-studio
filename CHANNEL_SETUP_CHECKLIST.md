Before you start connecting channels, complete these prerequisites:

- [ ] You have logged into DM Data Studio
- [ ] You've navigated to your project **Settings**
- [ ] You've enabled the channels you need in "Connected Channels"

## Quick Support

**Issue:** Connection keeps failing
**Quick Fix:** Try signing out completely from all Google/Facebook tabs, then reconnect

**Issue:** Data not showing
**Quick Fix:** Wait 2 minutes, refresh page, check that account has active content

**Issue:** Permission error
**Quick Fix:** Verify you have admin access, try different browser, clear cache

**Issue:** Can't find property/account/page
**Quick Fix:** Make sure you own/manage it, that it's active, and you're signed into the right account
